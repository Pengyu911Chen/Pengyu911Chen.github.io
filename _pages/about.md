I am currently an M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University. My research focuses on autonomous aerial robotics, with an emphasis on motion planning, optimal control, learning-based control, and spatial reasoning. I am particularly interested in bridging foundation models with classical robotics algorithms or learning-based control method to enable long-horizon autonomous navigation and real-time decision-making in previously unknown environments.
